Unit 1: Day in, Day out

Casharkan waxaan ku baran doonaa Present Simple iyo sida looga hadlo hab-maalmeedka (Daily Routine).

Vocabulary: Daily Activities

  • Get up: Tooraado / Hurdada ka kac.
  • Have breakfast: Quraaco.
  • Go to work: Shaqo tag.
  • Watch TV: Telefishinka daawo.

Grammar: Present Simple

Waxaan isticmaalnaa Present Simple marka aan ka hadlayno wax joogto ah (Habits).

Example: I get up at 7:00 AM every day.

Daawo Sharaxaadda Casharka

Muuqaalkani wuxuu si faahfaahsan u sharaxayaa casharka Unit 1.
